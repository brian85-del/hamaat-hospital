Laboratory

Our laboratory is dedicated to providing fast, accurate diagnostic testing to support your healthcare needs. Backed by modern equipment and experienced lab technicians, we deliver reliable results that help your doctor make the right call, quickly.

  • Full blood count, chemistry & hormone panels
  • Diabetes & cholesterol screening
  • Same-day results for most standard tests
  • Home sample collection available

Operation Theatre

Our operation theatre is designed to provide safe, efficient, and high-quality surgical care. From minor procedures to more involved surgeries, our surgical team and support staff follow strict safety protocols at every step.

  • Fully equipped, sterile surgical suite
  • Experienced surgeons & anaesthetists
  • Pre- and post-operative monitoring
  • Flexible payment plans available

Geriatric Care

Our Geriatric Care services are focused on providing specialised attention and support for the elderly in the comfort of their own homes, helping families keep loved ones safe, comfortable, and well cared for.

  • Mobility & fall-risk assessments
  • Medication management
  • Chronic condition monitoring
  • Companionship & daily living support

Specialist Consultation

Our specialist consultation service provides expert assessments and personalised treatment plans tailored to your healthcare needs, connecting you with the right doctor for your condition.

  • Paediatric, geriatric & general specialists
  • In-depth diagnosis & treatment planning
  • Referrals for advanced care where needed
  • Follow-up visits included

Home Care

Our home care service offers professional, personalised healthcare in the comfort of your home — ideal for recovery after a procedure, chronic care management, or ongoing nursing support.

  • Qualified home nursing visits
  • Wound care & injections
  • Vital signs monitoring
  • Flexible scheduling, 7 days a week

Maternal Care

Our maternal care unit provides full support through every stage of pregnancy, childbirth, and postnatal care, so every mother feels informed, safe, and supported.

  • Prenatal check-ups & ultrasounds
  • Safe delivery support
  • Postnatal & newborn care
  • Breastfeeding & wellness guidance
