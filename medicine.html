Important Legal & Medical Notice
  • 1. Not a Substitute: This service is for inquiry and information purposes only. It does not replace a consultation with a licensed healthcare provider.
  • 2. Prescription Medicines: Under Kenyan law, prescription-only medicines require a valid prescription. All prescriptions are verified by a licensed pharmacist before processing.
  • 3. Legal Compliance: Hamat Healthcare operates in accordance with the Pharmacy and Poisons Act (Cap 244) and PPB guidelines.
  • 4. Privacy: Your information is handled per the Data Protection Act, 2019.
